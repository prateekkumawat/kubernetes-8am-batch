Kubernetes schduling ::::: 
- pod distribution management is manage by kubernetes schduler. 
- that schduler is decided on ehich node your pod will be run and availabel 
- kubernetes schduler is decided to manage 

3 way to manage schdule 
- NodeSelector 
- Taint and Tolerations 
- Affinity 

Schduleing managemnt working on Label based... 
Label key:value

NodeSelector ::: 
- label based node selection 
- just match label and schdule that 
-  nodeSelector is the simplest recommended form of node selection constraint

kubectl label nodes <your-node-name> disktype=ssd
